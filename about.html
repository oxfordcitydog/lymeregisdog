About Lyme Regis Dog

The story behind the site, and the community that keeps it growing.

Hi, I'm Jane — I made this site because I wanted a simple, honest guide to the walks, beaches, shops, cafes, accommodation and services around Lyme Regis that actually welcome dogs, written by the people who take their dogs there, not a directory of guesses.

Every review on this site comes from a real visit by a real dog and their owner — including my own dog, Oak, who's clocked up a fair few of the reviews you'll see around the site. There's no algorithm deciding what's "dog-friendly" here, just people sharing what they've found.

If you've discovered somewhere your dog loves, I'd genuinely love for you to add it to the map. The more people who contribute, the more useful this becomes for everyone bringing their dog to Lyme Regis — that's the whole point of it.

Found something wrong, out of date, or a review that shouldn't be here? Get in touch via the social links on the homepage and I'll sort it.
