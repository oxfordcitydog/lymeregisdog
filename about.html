About Lyme Regis Dog

The story behind the site, and the community that keeps it growing.

Hi, we're Jane and Oak — a mature lady who loves exploring (but consistently gets lost) and her senior black Labrador.

We moved to Lyme Regis from Oxford City in November 2025, and one of the first things we wanted was a simple way to find dog-friendly walks, beaches, shops, cafes, accommodation and services nearby. That's exactly why this site exists.

Before this, I used to run a similar community review site back in Oxford called oxfordcitydog.com. When we moved, I switched all of that site's social media over to Lyme Regis Dog instead — so if you follow us anywhere, that history is why the accounts look the way they do.

This site is different from most directories you'll find online: I'm not trying to sell you anything. There are no pop-up ads, and there never will be. I personally review every single entry as it comes in — it's just me, a real human, checking things before they go live.

Found something wrong, out of date, or a review that shouldn't be here? Get in touch via the social links on the homepage and I'll sort it.

Above all, this site is about community first — real people and their dogs, sharing the places they genuinely love. If you've found somewhere special, please add it to the map. And if you enjoy the site, I'd love it if you shared it with other dog owners and followed along with Oak on social media — search lymeregisdog.

Thank you!
Jane & Oak 🐾
