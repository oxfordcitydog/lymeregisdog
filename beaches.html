Sample Review: The Monmouth Beach Coastal Path

A beautiful seaside stroll heading west from the Cobb, perfect for catching sea breezes and hunting for fossils along the coast.

Overall Paw Rating 🐾🐾🐾🐾☆ (4/5 Paws)
Older Dog Accessibility Easy to Moderate. Flat stretches but includes loose shingle paths that require careful footing.
Terrain Type Rough Terrain (Loose pebbles, tidal rocks & large stones)
Lead Requirements Off-lead friendly, but watch the tide lines carefully!
Oak's Ball-Throw Potential High! Plenty of wide-open beach spaces when the tide is out.
Human Refreshments Great dog-friendly kiosks nearby along the Cobb.

Sample Review: Limnhurst River Woodland Walk

A shaded, sheltered valley path walking inland alongside the calm river waters. Ideal for hot summer afternoons.

Overall Paw Rating 🐾🐾🐾🐾🐾 (5/5 Paws)
Older Dog Accessibility Perfect for Older Dogs. Wide, completely flat paths with plenty of rest benches along the riverway.
Terrain Type Maintained Path (Smooth, level gravel walkways)
Lead Requirements Leads recommended near the deeper river bends.
Oak's Ball-Throw Potential Medium (watch out for the riverbanks if your dog loves swimming!).
Human Refreshments Water bowls outside local town cafes on the route back.
