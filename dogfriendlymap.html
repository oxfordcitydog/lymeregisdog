🐾 Dog-Friendly Walk Mapper

Draw your dog's walk route and save it!

How to use:

  1. Click "Draw my walk" button
  2. Tap on the map to create your walk path — paw prints will appear
  3. To mark water spots, click "Mark water" then tap the map
  4. Use "Undo" to remove the last tap
  5. Use "Start over" to clear everything and begin again
  6. Type your dog's name and walk name below the map
  7. Click "Save my walk" to get a link to share
