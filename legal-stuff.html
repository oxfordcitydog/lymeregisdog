Safety Notice

Local terrain, cliff edges, coastal tides, and business rules can change rapidly. Please always obey local signage, keep your dog safe, and use this guide entirely at your own risk.

The heat guide on this site is provided for general information only and should not be relied upon as professional or veterinary advice. Weather and ground temperatures can vary significantly depending on location, surface type, shade, and time of day. Always check the pavement temperature yourself (for example, with the back of your hand for several seconds) and use your own judgement before walking your dog. If in doubt, wait until it is cooler.
