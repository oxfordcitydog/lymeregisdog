Angel Dogs

from lymeregisdog.com

Point your camera at a dog and a halo appears above them. Works on up to six dogs at once. Nothing is recorded and nothing leaves your phone.

The Style button swaps between a golden angel halo and a fiery devil one, for the dogs who rolled in something. Approved by Oak of Lyme Regis, who is of course an angel.

To keep Angel Dogs on your iPhone: tap the Share button (the square with an arrow, at the bottom of Safari), then choose "Add to Home Screen".

It looks like Angel Dogs is already on this device - look for Oak's icon on your home screen or with your other apps. (On a computer, there may also be a small install icon at the right-hand end of the address bar.) If you can't find it, open this page in Chrome or Edge and tap the button again.
